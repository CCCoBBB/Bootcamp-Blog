Foundations Reflection

What are three new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

I've learned that I don't get as easily stressed as I did in high-school. I managed my emotions and protected
my mental state by meditating and stopping when I felt like I couldn't keep pushing.

What are the role of values, empathy, and self-awareness in learning and programming?

Our values shape how we live our lives. Values shape our behaviour, attitudes and beliefs. What we value determine
how we learn and grow as developers.

Empathy enables us to communicate better, build stronger relationships and cooperate more efficiently with other
individual in our programming space.

Self-awareness helps us to recognize our strengths and weaknesses which we can then acknowledge and recieve help
based on what we are good at and what we are bad at.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

The effectiveness of taking a step back to meditate. I feel so much less of weight on my life in general.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

Learning how to think like a programmer is quite challenging. Learning to think in a different way was quite
confusing to try and do. I usually take on a problem at its face value and not break it down at all. Throughout
foundations, I've broken down problems into smaller problems and it's definitely helped.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on
core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

These core lessons help us transition from a student programmer into an efficient programming employee.
These lessons also prepare us for things like interviews and how to collaberate and communicate
with other programmers to be more productive.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you
have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

I think if we only worked on programming and how to code well, we would end up burnt out significantly
faster and out of a job too because we wouldn't perform very well as an employee. We wouldn't have good
soft skills that are needed for a job in this industry.
