  • Identify your strengths and how they will support you during your learning journey.
    • I did the CliftonStrengths assessment about 6 months ago at a retreat. I found my top 5 strengths were the following in this order:
    1. Activator - make things happen by turning thoughts into action. I want to do things now, rather than simply talk about them.
    2. Belief - I certain core values that are unchanging. Out of these values emerges a defined purpose for my life.
    3. Connectedness - I believe that things happen for a reason. (insignificant to this journey imo lol)
    4. Developer - I spot the signs of each small improvement and derive satisfaction from evidence of progress.
    5. Adaptability - enables me to respond willingly to the demands of the moment even if they pull me away from my plans. Unlike some, I don't resent sudden requests or unforeseen detours. I expect them. They are inevitable. On some level I actually look forward to them.
    I think my first and fourth strength will be very supporing in this journey. They both seem very beneficial to a life of learning to code.
